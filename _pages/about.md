I’m an evolutionary biologist at the [University of East Anglia](https://uea.ac.uk). My group and I study evolutionary adaptation in wild and lab populations. I'm also an associate editor of [Evolution](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/journal/15585646) and a UCU[](https://www.ucu.org.uk/) branch committee member. Here you can find information on our research group, research interests, and find details about any positions available working with us.
